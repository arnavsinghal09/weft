# Governance

Weft is currently a **single-maintainer project** (BDFL-style) in its
bootstrap phase. This document records how decisions are made today and how
that changes as the project grows.

## Today

- The maintainer has final say on design, scope, and releases.
- Substantial decisions (architecture, language, naming, license policy) are
  recorded in `PROJECT_NOTES.md` or `docs/` so they are argued in writing,
  not folklore. Reversing a recorded decision requires a written rationale in
  the same place.
- Anyone may propose changes via issues and PRs; see `CONTRIBUTING.md`.

## Becoming a maintainer

Sustained, high-quality contributions (code, review, triage) over a few
months lead to an invitation to co-maintain. Maintainers get merge rights and
share release duty.

## When there are 3+ maintainers

This document will be replaced with a lightweight consensus model:
lazy consensus on PRs, simple maintainer majority for contested decisions,
and a public record of votes in the relevant issue. Until then, this stub is
honest about how things actually work.

## Conduct and security

Code-of-conduct enforcement and security response are maintainer duties; see
`CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md` and `SECURITY.md` for contact channels.
